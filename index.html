So What Are We Building?

It’s an app for TEDx conferences, and it solves the problem of inconsistencies of different platforms in the ways of registration of participants, running administration and managing all TEDx conferences around the world.

The main role of the application is providing a unified interface for organisers. Basic functionality will include the following:

  • 1. full support for participants management,
  • 2. different ways of registrations,
  • 3. filtering and searching speakers,
  • 4. data analysing and business intelligence,
  • 5. run entire events from beginning to after event surveys.


The goal is to help organisers save time and automatize all process with TEDx team - little touches make for happier speakers and participants, which means better reviews and a good brand.

Design



Design is an iterative process. There are sketches, wireframes, mockups and eventually some form of 'final' design.

We looked at a variety of ways to solve the design problems. At first, we defined basic main features of the app and logically organise them into one flow. After that, we designed the basic layout and menu of the application.





1. User Flow

Create a new canvas . The best way is starting to create the basic flow of the app on paper. You can sketch all variations and follow your creative mind. Here you can see our first basic sketch of our app layout.





2. Wireframes

After the first feedback round and once users are happy with the user flow, the fun starts. We started wireframing our design from paper using UXPin to make wireframes the best and quickest way.
Once we have our final wireframe, we finished the last touch very easy for the next step of our workflow.

Development


Development is an iterative process. We are making design and development together. We use front-end First development method, where we made design and made changes directly in the code or debugger in console. We can quickly react to user feedback and show them real app without the full logic of the code in the back-end.

Our solution and main features

  • 1. Single interface to manage all TEDx conferences
  • 2. Automate repetitive processes
  • 3. Manage TEDx team, speakers, talks and volunteers from one single platform
  • 4. Participants, payments, registrations and sponsorships reports
  • 5. Duplicate regular activities, manage the entire run before the conference, during the conference and after the conference
  • 6. Full data analysis, report questionnaires and feedback from the speakers and participants
